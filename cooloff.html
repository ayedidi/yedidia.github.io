Overview

This video was made in collaboration with L00Z, a musical and visual artist. This song in particular was intended to showcase her handpainted, modified clothing designs.

Art Direction

Each of the L00Z models is wearing a custom L00Z clothing item. L00Z also provided the text credits at the end of the video.

3D Art

All 3D art and animation in this music video was created by me in Blender.

Character Design

The main character in the video is a 3D modeled version of L00Z.
