Process

I created the game while taking a Virtual Reality Specialization certificate course from Goldsmith's University of London.

The game takes place at a parfait cafe that uses 3D models of the chef (you), your work station, customers, ingredients, and your boss. The goal of the stakes in this game is to keep the player highly stimulated. The medium is the Oculus Quest HMD.

As fruits run around your workstation, you are to catch them, add them to the correct order, .

The player uses the triggers on the quest controller to pick up cups, utensils, and fruits.

Influence and Inspiration

My imagined app takes elements from Job Simulator, Cook-Out, and Takelings House Party.

This game is suited for immersion as the player feels they are someone else within this imaginary hectic kitchen.
